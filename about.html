Who We Are

At Ertugul Enterprises, we are passionate about creating beautiful homes, harmonious workspaces and welcoming lifestyle hubs. We are invested in our journey of co-creating the future with our consumers so that they can explore and engage with life more meaningfully.

We stay true to our purpose of curating living spaces for the health and happiness of communities. This means designing an ecosystem where relationships are nurtured and enhanced.

We have built our culture and capabilities around continuously evolving consumer tastes and aspirations. It is here, through our unwavering commitment, that we are able to bring boundless urban living to life.

Our Mission

We aim to be development expert and leader in the field of retail, commercial and residential spaces. With our objective to fulfill all our customer's needs, we strive to create 'quality' properties, which offer long-term benefits to the community.

Our Vision

We envisage the following:

Customer service: As a company, we visualize our customer to be our greatest assets hence their satisfaction is our biggest aim. We make all our efforts in the direction of offering valued services to each of our customers in a cost-effective, timely and value-added manner.

Employees satisfaction: We support and encourage our employees with reward systems. Our company believes in fostering an employee-friendly environment that permits the employees to show entrepreneurship and leadership behavior among them.

Communication: Through customer engagement in all our projects, we encourage customer participation in work processes. Constant communication helps us to fetch necessary information from the clients and to accomplish our jobs at the level of customer satisfaction.

Training and Development: We believe that constant learning and training helps in business development hence we encourage training of our employees at various stages and try to build a highly updated organization.
